Diversity

Diversity measures how dissimilar recommended items are for a user. This similarity is often determined using the items content (e.g.Hotel proposed) but can also be determined using how similarly items are rated. One measure of diversity is the Intra-List Similarity (ILS). The ILS equation can calculate the similarity between any two items (ij, ik) using the cosine similarity, Jaccard similarity coefficient, or another similarity metric could be utilized in the equation.
